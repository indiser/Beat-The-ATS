Job Title: Senior DevOps Cloud Engineer

Job Summary:
We are looking for a highly technical engineer to deploy microservices on AWS. You will build CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins and GitLab. You must be proficient in Linux, Bash scripting, and containerization tools.

Technical Requirements:
* Must have strong experience with Docker and Kubernetes orchestration.
* Proficiency in Python, Golang, or Rust.
* Deep understanding of TCP/IP, DNS, and HTTP protocols.
* Experience with Terraform or Ansible for infrastructure as code.
* Ability to configure Nginx and Apache servers.
* Knowledge of NoSQL databases like MongoDB or Cassandra.

Education:
* Masters in Computer Science or Electrical Engineering.
* AWS Certified Solutions Architect preferred.
