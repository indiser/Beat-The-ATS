Job Title: Special Needs Program Coordinator & Counseling Supervisor

Job Summary: We are seeking a dedicated professional with four years experience in early childhood development and a diverse background in the care of special needs children and adults. The ideal candidate will have prior experience as a Counseling Supervisor or Client Specialist.



Key Responsibilities: * Coordinate service assignments for counselors and client families. * Manage and oversee volunteer workers. * Determine work placement for special needs adult clients and maintain client databases and records. * Coordinate client contact with local health care professionals on a monthly basis. * Oversee daily activity and outing planning for clients. * Assist families with researching financial assistance and healthcare. * Assist teachers with managing daily classroom activities.





Education & Requirements: * Must have a BS in Early Childhood Development. * Must have a BA in Elementary Education. * Previous experience as a Teacher's Assistant is preferred.
